    Both span and div are used to group content in a document or page and further bring no adherent value. They both mark a section in a document
    Difference between span and div
    Div's are used to show larger parts of text and sections of the documents. They span entire blocks of content. Span's are used to denote smaller sections in the paragraph of document. Multiple spans can be nested inside divs, while the opposite does not hold true.
